Thesis:
PlayVerse presents a complete full-stack academic game store: public discovery, secure access, private catalog administration, community features and a SQL Server-backed domain model.

Audience:
Teachers and evaluators on exposition day.

One-line arc:
The presentation moves from the problem of managing a game store to the implemented solution, technologies, architecture, screenshots and team conclusions.
